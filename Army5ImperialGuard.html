Imperial Guard

The Imperial Guard are simple human soldiers. They are the not the best equipped soldiers of the Imperium of Man but that is not important when you have trillions of soldiers throughout the galaxy.

Unit Name Description models per unit
Command Squad An officer and his assistants. 5
Trooper Squad Basic infantry squad with short range fire power. 10 to 20
Field Ordinance Battery Medium range artillery. 1 to 3
Scout Walker Light armored scout walker with a flamethrower and chainsaw. 1 to 3
Armored Walker A walker with heavier armor and weapons used for anti-vehicle role. 1 to 3
Infantry Squad A squad of infantry with a heavy weapon for long range fire support. 10
