The Necrons

A race of ancient aliens that once controlled the galaxy. They were tricked into becoming soulless metal robots and went into a long hibernation after being defeated by the other ancient races of the galaxy. They have now awoken from their long rest and are now trying to reclaim their galactic empire. Currently need to finish painting some models.
